About StatChaser

I created StatChaser because I wanted a way to visualize how a player was doing if only their last N games were counted. I think this a valuable way to look at player data because it allows the user to ignore games early in a player's career that might not be relevant to the level that they are playing closer to the present. The chart component of StatChaser makes this possible and the additional tables underneath give the user an idea of how a player is performing currently in relation to his entire careeer.
